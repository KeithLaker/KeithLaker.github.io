What is Graph Analytics?

Graph algorithms enable you to explore and discover relationships in social networks, IoT, big data, data warehouses and complex transaction data for applications such as fraud detection in banking, customer 360, and smart manufacturing.

Oracle Graph includes 50 built-in, parallel analytic functions to perform ranking, centrality, recommendation, community detection, path finding, and more. These functions provide highly scalable, fast query execution to speed up the discovery of relationships within your data sets.
