Oracle Global Leaders - Feedback Sessions 2023

The Oracle Global Leaders feedback sessions are virtual group meetings that take place twice a year to share opinions with Database Executive Management, Database Product Management, and Database Development Leads.





Overview of our feedback program


To make sure we stay tuned into market trends and ever-changing customer needs, we are reaching out to select customers who are willing to work with us to receive detailed feedback on our products, services, roadmap and help identify ways to work better together in areas related to the Oracle Database.

Together with Database Executive Management, select Product Management and Database Development Leads, we will meet virtually as a group twice a year virtually. In these sessions we will share opinions and as we may have follow up questions for you individually, we would value time with you one-on-one at your convenience.

Some benefits of giving your feedback:

  • Address your feedback and requirements directly to Oracle Executive Management
  • Direct access to Oracle Development and Product Management teams
  • Understand Oracle´s Roadmap and New Developments
  • Regular Product Updates and Seminars delivered by Product Development

How the feedback sessions are organized


The virtual feedback sessions are separated into three categories:

  • Oracle Database Development
    • Building applications using the Oracle Database
    • SQL and programming languages (Java, JavaScript, Python, etc.)
    • In-Database programming languages (PL/SQL, JavaScript, etc.)
    • REST APIs, CI/CD Pipelines, Development Tools
    • Microservices & Cloud-Native
    • APEX
    • ML/AI & Data Science
  • Oracle Database Management System & Exadata
    • Database Security
    • Patching / Upgrading
    • Fleet Management
    • Maximum Availability Architecture (MAA)
    • Converged Database
    • Exadata
  • Oracle Database in the Cloud
    • Multi-Cloud Architecture
    • Security, Regulatory Requirements
    • Fleet Management
    • Migration to the Cloud
    • Competition

Your feedback will be addressed directly to the Executive Vice Presidents of Oracle Database Development and their staff:

  • Andy Mendelsohn - EVP - Database Server Technologies
  • Juan Loaiza - EVP - Systems Technology
  • Çetin Özbütün - EVP - DW and Autonomous Database Technologies

Meeting Agenda


We do not have a formal set agenda as this is typically based on YOUR valued input, and follows a specific process, such as:

Typical preparation tasks:
  • Identify customers for each session (Oracle)
  • Conduct customer interviews using a questionnaire template for each session (Oracle, Customers)
  • Identify session topics based on questionnaire/interview feedback from customers (Oracle)
  • Prepare presentation content for each customer per identified topics (Oracle, Customers)
Feedback Event:

Share feedback on different topics directly with the product development owners and product management contacts, based on the slides created (Customers)

Post-meeting follow up:

Follow-up sessions for individual customers or groups based on the discussions during the feedback sessions (Oracle, Customers)

How to sign-up for Oracle Global Leaders Feedback Sessions 2023


The Global Leaders Feedback Sessions are not designed as a one-off. We would like to establish a long and close relationship with our feedback customers.

Customers who are not able or willing to provide feedback for a specific year or event are invited to attend the session as listeners and learn from the feedback of other customers.

If this all sounds interesting to you, please get in contact with us via email to sign up or for further questions and comments.
