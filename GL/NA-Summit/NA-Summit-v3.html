Oracle Global Leaders Summer Meeting Americas 2020

Sept 2 - Sept 3 | 1:00pm EST - 5:00pm EST

Welcome

For this Americas virtual summer meeting the focus is around product updates from the Oracle database product management teams and a series of customer panels with Q&A sessions afterwards on the following topics:

  • - Oracle Autonomous Database
  • - Cloud Architectures for Data Warehousing
  • - Machine Learning and Data Science
  • - Exadata Architectures for Data Management
  • - Easy And Low Code Development
  • - Analytics With ADW Deployment
