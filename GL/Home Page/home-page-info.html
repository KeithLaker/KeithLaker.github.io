OVERVIEW

The Global Leaders Program is a customer program, aimed at establishing a close and trustful, long term relationship with Information Management customers worldwide.

The team from the Oracle DW & Big Data Product Management group maintains a networking group of customers implementing these technologies, keeping them in touch, up to date with new features and providing insights and guidance on solutions and sometimes troubleshooting on implementations.

Through this program and the contact with the Development Organization and peers, Global Leaders members are successful in their data management solutions and better enabled to maintain their leading edge. Contact laura.j.mckechnie@oracle.com for more info.

BENEFITS

There are many benefits in being part of this global program, including:

  • Oracle Global Leaders 2 Day events
  • Oracle Global Leaders Webinars
  • Day with Development deep dive one day classes
  • Business & Technical Roundtables
  • Access to previous event collateral and sharing of customer peers solutions
  • Special Leaders Groups
  • ADW Champions who meet virtually an inperson twice a year
  • Early Adopter Access
  • Architecture advice, review and health checks
  • Establish relationship with Oracle Development
  • Customer Advisory Board participation
  • Partnership with Oracle at an executive level
  • Oracle Excellence Awards preference

APPLY

For more information and details of how to joing this program, contact laura.j.mckechnie@oracle.com.

EVENTS

We hold regular gatherings, training sessions and Day-with-Development workshops in all regions and one worldwide meeting during Oracle Open World. There are monthly virtual meetups on hot topics and web conferences of customer`s implementations for members to  keep in touch and learn from other client´s experience.

These events allow our members to share experience and best practice for implementing Data Warehouse and Big Data projects, and indeed all data management solutions, in the Cloud or on premise.
