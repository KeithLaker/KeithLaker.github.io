Oracle Global Leaders Summer Meeting EMEA 2020

June 23 - June 24 | 13:00 CET - 18:00 CET

Welcome

For this EMEA summer meeting the focus is around product updates from the Oracle product management teams and a series of customer panels with Q&A sessions afterwards. The customer panel topics are:

  • - Oracle Cloud Infrastructure
  • - Oracle Autonomous Database
  • - Oracle Exadata for Analytics
  • - Oracle Analytics
  • - Oracle Machine Learning
  • - Oracle Database Development
 

NOTE: the links for watching the recordings and downloading the presentations for each session will become active after the event.
