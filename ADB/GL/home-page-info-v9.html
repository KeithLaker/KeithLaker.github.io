Oracle Global Leaders

The Oracle Global Leaders Program is an elite program for our global big data warehousing and cloud customers. It is managed by the Oracle data warehousing product management team and serves to establish an open communication channel for providing product insights, solution planning and implementation reviews between Oracle Development and customers who are at the leading-edge of big data warehousing initiatives. It also provides a rich opportunity for these customers to network with their peers through regional and global communities.

Program Overview

  • The Global Leaders Program is a customer program, aimed at establishing a close and trustful, long term relationship with Information Management customers worldwide.

  • The team from the Oracle DW & Big Data Product Management group maintains a networking group of customers implementing these technologies, keeping them in touch, up to date with new features and providing insights and guidance on solutions and sometimes troubleshooting on implementations.

  • Through this program and the contact with the Development Organization and peers, Global Leaders members are successful in their data management solutions and better enabled to maintain their leading edge. Contact laura.j.mckechnie@oracle.com for more info.

Program Benefits

  • There are many benefits in being part of this global program, including:

    • Complimentary in-person 2 Day events in select locations (when restrictions allow) hosted by Product Management and Intel
    • Champions Groups –Database and ADB champions virtual meetups held monthly
    • Direct From Development Webinars, with handpicked topics, held quarterly -everything you need to know for lakehouse, data warehousing and analytics solutions
    • Virtual Architecture and Performance Checkups – a 3 hour- scoped assessment with guidance from our technical team
    • Database Feedback Sessions with Oracle Executive Database Management – every 6 months - it’s a two way street and we listen!
    • Early Adopter listing information and access to new features
    • 1:1 introductions of customer and partner peers
    • Highlight and publicize customer and partner successes, in partnership with Oracle

    For more information and details of how to join this program, contact laura.j.mckechnie@oracle.com.
