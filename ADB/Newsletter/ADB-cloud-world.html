Are you and your customers coming to Cloud World?

We have an exciting and packed agenda for you at this year's Cloud World, with over 100 Autonomous Database sessions and workshops.

1

FACE-TO-FACE MEETINGS WITH PRODUCT MANAGEMENT

During the event, there will be a CVC area offering rooms for face-to-face meetings where we can meet you and your customers to discuss use cases of how and where to use Autonomous Database, provide insight about the long-term feature roadmap, and discuss key programs such as our Global Leaders community and our strategic customer support program.
